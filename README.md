To start this project, I was interested in how I should be investing in the future. I decided to look at stocks from 2013 to 2018 and learn about what is happening in the tech world. Overall, something that was interesting to me that I learned was that even from 2013 to 2018, Nvidia went up a lot. I thought that a lot of its increases were in the last couple of years due to AI, but it started a long time ago. Also, another one that was interesting to look at was the fact that Netflix went up so much. It does make sense, though, since it was the first real streaming service. In section two, I learned about the biggest losers. This was something I also wanted to study because I wanted to remind myself that stocks don’t always go up. It taught me about the years in which stocks saw the biggest daily average return decline. Broadcom, Google, and Apple got hit hard. -0.2 average daily return was fascinating to see. Broadcom has seen a major increase since this, and I don’t know if I would be able to hold through that sort of decline. The third section taught me about the average daily return of stocks in the last 90 days of the dataset. It told me that Qualcomm, Netflix, and Texas Instruments would see upside following this period. This is interesting to see because a lot of these stocks have done well. The only time that really popped off was Netflix, though. This showed me that you should always go with the top growth stock, right? Usually, when you ride on a wave, it is about to crash, but if you hop on it a way out, you can ride it for a while. Lastly, I learned the stocks that were projected to lose based on the last 90 days. It told me Micron Technology, Broadcom, and AMD. I thought this was especially interesting because these seem to be some of the stocks that have gone up the most in recent years. This really goes to show you that you can never predict the market. Overall, I think what I learned from this project is to take the safe bet. Overall, learning from a past dataset was interesting because I thought it would be a total showing of the stocks that would go down in the future. It’s interesting to see that people once thought chip manufacturers weren’t the future, but that’s totally changed. I think people had thought that we had reached a point where our technology was getting good in the chip world. The rise of AI has made it, so these resources are incredibly valuable. I think that if I want to invest in my future, I must think about the things that will do well in the future rather than thinking about what will do well right now. As I said before, you must find the wave before it arrives to maximize gains.

Basically, I think that looking at these files, you should understand that you can’t beat the market unless you get lucky. Nobody ever knows what is going to be popular in the future, and most professional stockbrokers don’t beat the market.
